## HOOK COLUMN - CONDITIONAL LOGIC
This column depends **entirely** on whether `"Hook"` was selected in **Structure Elements**.

* **IF `"Hook"` IS in your Structure Elements list:**
  * Select **ONE** hook type from: `Question`, `Statistical or Fact`, `Quotation`, `Anecdotal or Story`, `Personal or Emotional`
  * **Example Structure Elements:** `"H3, Lists, Hook, Conclusion, FAQ"`
  * **Example Introductory Hook:** `"Question"`

* **IF `"Hook"` is NOT in your Structure Elements list:**
  * **MUST** output: `"None"`
  * **Example Structure Elements:** `"H3, Tables, Lists, Conclusion"`
  * **Example Introductory Hook:** `"None"`

**CRITICAL:** Do not specify a hook type if Hook is **not** listed in Structure Elements. The two columns must match.

### Examples:
**CORRECT:**
* `Structure Elements: "Hook, H3, Lists, Conclusion"` | `Introductory Hook: "Statistical or Fact"`
* `Structure Elements: "Tables, H3, Lists, FAQ"` | `Introductory Hook: "None"`

**WRONG:**
* `Structure Elements: "Tables, H3, Lists, FAQ"` | `Introductory Hook: "Question"` ← *Hook not in structure!*
* `Structure Elements: "Hook, Lists, Conclusion, H3"` | `Introductory Hook: "None"` ← *Hook IS in structure!*
